Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embracing new ideas and technologies to drive progress and efficiency in all sectors of society.

Sustainability

Promoting practices that protect the environment and ensure long-term viability for future generations.

Infrastructure

Building robust digital and physical infrastructure to support economic growth and connectivity.

Empowerment

Enabling citizens through education, technology access, and economic opportunities for inclusive development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable, eco-friendly, and prosperous future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"We must build a Bangladesh where technology serves humanity, where innovation drives inclusive growth, and where every citizen has the opportunity to contribute to our nation's prosperity. The vision of Bangladesh 2.0 is not just about economic development, but about creating a society where social justice, environmental sustainability, and human dignity are at the center of all our endeavors."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
